About Monochrome Midnight

Monochrome Midnight is a R&B group, that occasionally dabbles into Pop. They're known for their emotional performances and soothing sound. Formed in 2023, the band had hit 6.7 million streams for their debut album and later went on to tour across Europe. They continue to grow a passionate fanbase worldwide, leading to their next upcoming tour across Asia.
